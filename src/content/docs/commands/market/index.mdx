---
title: Market
description: View, manage, find and buy market Pokémon
---

If you are looking for a Pokémon to buy, use \`/market search\` (and use the filters) to find a Pokémon you want.
After finding the Pokémon you want, use \`/market view\` to view the Pokémon in detail.
If you like the Pokémon and want to buy it, use \`/market buy\` to buy it.

If you want to sell your Pokémon, use \`/market create\` to sell your Pokémon.
You can set the price and the Pokémon will be listed on the market for other players to buy.
To view all your Pokémon for sale on the market, use \`/market listings\`!
